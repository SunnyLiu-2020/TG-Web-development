The practices with regard to single or double quotes in JavaScript seem to be contradictory. On the one hand editors and textbooks seem to use " " as default. But on the other hand according to this article as shared by Jens in Slack ' ' is more common (Should I use 'single' or "double-quotes" for strings in JavaScript). One good reason to use single quotation marks is the fact that you don't need to press the Shift key every time.
